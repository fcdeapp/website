What Makes Abrody Different

Platform → User⇄User → Platform

We flip the direction of learning. It no longer flows from the platform to the user. With Abrody, everything starts from you — your situations, your context, your words.

Learn From Your Own Documents

Upload your proposals, papers, and slides. Abrody’s AI extracts key points and core vocabulary, then turns them into repeatable practice.

AI-Powered Summaries & Drills

No more manual note-taking. Abrody auto-generates concise summaries and targeted quizzes from your real work.

More Natural Voice Learning

Abrody’s voices sound real—so you can listen, repeat, and practice in real-world scenarios, anytime, anywhere.

Instant AI Dialogs From Your Files

Jump into natural AI conversations based on the content you actually need—emails, reports, even presentations.

Progress That Transfers to Work

Every session is grounded in your actual job context, so what you practice transfers to real workplace results.

Context First

Situation, intent, and phrasing come from your day—so it transfers.

Hyper-Personalization

Your mistakes become targeted quizzes. Your phrases become practice.

Less Game, More Gain
