The Problem

Why Most Language Apps Don’t Really Work

Apps push scripted content at you. But fluency comes from your own moments and words — not someone else’s.

Generic Lessons

Prewritten drills don’t fit your life or goals.

One-Way Learning

Content flows from platform to user — not the other way around.

Low Retention

When it’s irrelevant, you stop returning — and stop improving.

Our Approach

How Abrody Changes Everything

We flip the direction of learning: everything starts from you — your situations, context, and words.

User-Origin Learning

Real conversations seed lessons and reviews.

Context at the Core

Practice maps to your life, so it transfers to speaking.

Hyper-Personalization

Your mistakes → targeted quizzes; your phrases → focused practice.

AI-Powered CTL Loop

AI turns moments into dialogue, feedback, and quizzes that compound.
